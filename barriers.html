Reach — How We Solve

Get Seen, In Context

  • Active choice requirement — surface where merchants already are (Payouts, Payments dashboard). Force a clear decision. Collect feedback on "no." Critical for learning.
  • Contextual entry points — surface where instant fund availability matters (stores, bookings, restaurants).
  • New entry points — embed in Wix general flows (Payouts page, Welcome page, Demo site).
  • VIP customer outreach — targeted campaigns for high-value merchants.
  • Eligibility expansion — increase the addressable pool beyond current 75K.

Convince — Add Value

Make the Offer Worth It

  • Emphasize instant access — earnings available now vs. 2-3 day wait. Already live, under-communicated.
  • Subscription credits for adopters — discounts on Wix services. Tangible, immediate incentive.
  • Balance rewards (~1.5% APY) — earn on every dollar held.
Value gets attention.
But four fears still block the “yes.”
Fear 1

"It's a Bank Account"

Merchants hear "checking account" and think bank. They don't want another bank — they want to get paid.

How we dissolve it
  • Test Wix Balance messaging — A/B test naming and content. Squarespace positions a similar product as "Balance." Test whether the naming shift improves conversion.
  • Position as a payout wallet — not a bank account. Just a better way to receive and manage earnings.
Fear 2

"My Money Gets Stuck"

The #1 rational objection. Merchants fear losing control of cash flow if money enters a Wix account.

How we dissolve it
  • Auto-payout to external bank by default — configurable percentage, daily/weekly/monthly. Money flows through. No lock-in.
  • Merchants keep the speed advantage — instant access during the day, auto-transfer at their pace.
Fear 3

"I Don't Want a Debit Card"

The debit card signals commitment to a new financial product. It feels permanent.

How we dissolve it
  • Virtual-only by default — no physical card unless explicitly requested.
  • De-emphasize the card — it's a bonus, not an obligation.
Fear 4

"I Don't Know What This Is"

Some merchants are wary of the unknown. They don't understand what changes or what they're signing up for.

How we dissolve it
  • Shadow / preview mode — see the balance dashboard before committing. Build familiarity first.
  • Progressive education — show how earnings flow, what changes, what stays the same.

Activate — How We Solve

Make It Effortless

  • One-click onboarding — account opens in one tap.
  • Virtual card by default — physical card issuance moved to post-onboarding.
  • Security steps deferred — SSN, business info collected gradually after activation.
