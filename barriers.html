Where We Lose Them

75K eligible. 1.7K converted. Four drops, three problem types. Each needs a different strategy.

Reach — Drop 1
39%

Never see it

29K eligible merchants haven't encountered the product at all. Pure visibility problem.

How We Solve
  • Contextual entry points — surface where instant fund availability matters (stores, bookings, restaurants).
  • VIP customer outreach — targeted campaigns for high-value merchants.
  • Active choice requirement — force a clear decision. Collect feedback when merchants say "no." Critical for learning.
Convince — Drops 2 & 3
72%+ 77%

Saw it, said no

72% of exposed users aren't engaged. Of those who reach the welcome screen, 77% don't proceed. The value isn't compelling enough, and the product triggers anxiety.

How We Solve
1. Add Value
  • Emphasize instant access — earnings available now vs. 2-3 day wait. Already live, under-communicated.
  • Subscription credits for adopters — discounts on Wix services. Tangible, immediate incentive.
  • Balance rewards (~1.5% APY) — earn on every dollar held. Competitive with Shopify Balance.
2. Dissolve Fears — Four Hypotheses
"It's a bank account" — Test Wix Balance messaging. Squarespace positions a similar product as "Balance." A/B test naming impact on conversion.
"My money gets stuck" — Auto-payout to external bank by default (configurable % — daily/weekly/monthly). No lock-in.
"Debit card commitment" — Virtual-only by default. Card is a bonus, not an obligation.
"I don't know what this is" — Shadow/preview mode. See the balance dashboard before committing. Build familiarity first.
Activate — Drop 4
44%

Decided yes, didn't finish

44% who click "Open Account" drop during execution. They wanted it — we lost them at the door.

How We Solve
  • One-click onboarding — account opens in one tap.
  • Virtual card by default — physical card issuance moved to post-onboarding.
  • Security steps deferred — SSN, business info collected gradually after activation.

Reach. Convince. Activate.

But one barrier remains that no product change can solve: familiarity bias. "My setup works fine."

The answer isn't another feature. It's making this the default — once there's nothing left to fear or fight.

The Fifth Barrier — Familiarity Bias

Once every fear and friction is dissolved — change the default.
